About PersTenas Art Gallery

Founded in 2015 by renowned art curator Dr. Alejandra PersTenas, our gallery has become a beacon for contemporary art lovers and collectors worldwide. What started as a small exhibition space in the heart of the Creative Quarter has blossomed into a three-floor cultural destination, hosting over 50 exhibitions annually and representing more than 80 artists from 25 countries.

Our Story

The Beginning

Dr. Alejandra PersTenas spent 15 years working with major museums and galleries across Europe before returning to her hometown with a vision: to create a space where emerging artists could thrive alongside established masters, where innovation met tradition, and where art was accessible to everyone, not just the elite.

The gallery opened its doors on a rainy November afternoon in 2015 with just 12 artworks and a handful of visitors. Word quickly spread about the quality of the curation and the warmth of the welcome. Within a year, we had expanded to a second floor and established our now-famous artist residency program.

Growth and Evolution

As our reputation grew, so did our ambitions. In 2018, we launched our international artist exchange program, bringing creators from Tokyo to SÃ£o Paulo to showcase their work. In 2020, despite global challenges, we pioneered virtual gallery tours and online exhibitions, reaching audiences in over 60 countries.

Today, PersTenas Art Gallery stands as a testament to the power of art to connect, inspire, and transform. Our 15,000 square foot space includes three exhibition halls, a sculpture garden, artist studios, a conservation lab, and an educational center where we host workshops, lectures, and community events.

Our Mission

At PersTenas, we believe art is not a luxury but a necessity. Our mission encompasses three core pillars:

  1. Artist Empowerment: We provide artists with fair compensation, creative freedom, and the resources they need to push boundaries. Our 70/30 commission split favors the artist, and we never demand exclusivity.
  2. Collector Education: We demystify the art world, helping new collectors build confidence while providing seasoned collectors with access to emerging talents before they become household names.
  3. Community Engagement: Art belongs to everyone. Our free public programming, school partnerships, and subsidized exhibition access ensure that financial barriers never prevent someone from experiencing the transformative power of art.

Our Curators

Our curatorial team brings together over 100 years of combined experience in the art world. Each member has worked with major institutions including MoMA, Tate Modern, Centre Pompidou, and the Guggenheim. They travel extensively, attending art fairs, visiting studios, and maintaining relationships with artists, ensuring that PersTenas remains at the forefront of contemporary art.

Support Staff

Behind every successful exhibition is a dedicated team of art handlers, installation specialists, registrars, conservators, and administrative professionals. Our 25-person staff ensures that every detail, from lighting to labeling, meets the highest standards.

Programs and Services

Artist Residency Program

Our flagship program provides up to six artists per year with studio space, materials budget, living accommodations, and a guaranteed solo exhibition. Past residents have gone on to represent their countries at the Venice Biennale, win major awards, and achieve international recognition.

Educational Workshops

We offer workshops for all ages and skill levels, from children's art camps to professional development for emerging artists. Our master classes with renowned artists fill up months in advance.

Current Workshop Series:

  • Introduction to Abstract Painting (8 weeks)
  • Sculpture Fundamentals with James Thornhill
  • Digital Art for Beginners
  • Photography Masterclass: Composition and Light
  • Art History Lecture Series
  • Youth Art Explorers (ages 8-14)

Corporate Art Consultation

We help businesses build meaningful art collections that reflect their values and enhance their spaces. Our consultation services include needs assessment, artist selection, installation, and ongoing collection management.

Conservation Services

Our state-of-the-art conservation lab provides professional restoration and preservation services. Whether you have a damaged heirloom or a contemporary piece in need of maintenance, our certified conservators employ the latest techniques to protect your investment.

Sustainability Commitment

We recognize our responsibility to the environment and future generations. Our sustainability initiatives include:

  • Solar panels providing 60% of our energy needs
  • LED lighting throughout the gallery to reduce energy consumption
  • Recycled and sustainable materials for all exhibition installations
  • Carbon-neutral shipping for all artwork transportation
  • Water conservation systems in our sculpture garden
  • Zero-waste events policy
  • Partnership with artists working with recycled and reclaimed materials
