Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an appointment to visit?

No appointment is necessary during regular gallery hours. However, for private viewings, group tours (10+ people), or consultations with our curators, please contact us in advance to ensure availability.

Is there an admission fee?

General admission is $12 for adults, $8 for students and seniors, and free for children under 12. Every first Friday of the month features free admission for all visitors. Members enjoy complimentary admission year-round.

Can I take photographs in the gallery?

Personal photography without flash is welcome in most exhibition spaces. Some special exhibitions may have restrictions, which will be clearly marked. Commercial photography and video recording require advance permission.

How do I submit my artwork for consideration?

We review artist submissions quarterly. Please email artists@perstenas-art.com with your artist statement, CV, and 10-15 high-quality images of recent work. Include dimensions, materials, and year created for each piece. Due to the volume of submissions, we can only respond to artists we wish to schedule for a studio visit.

Do you offer art appraisal services?

We can provide informal opinions on artwork value, but for insurance or estate purposes, we recommend certified independent appraisers. We're happy to provide referrals to qualified professionals in your area.

Can I rent the gallery space for events?

Yes, our gallery spaces are available for private events, corporate functions, and special occasions. Rental includes access to our catering-approved kitchen, AV equipment, and flexible floor plans. Contact us for availability and pricing.

Directions and Parking

Public Transportation

  • Metro: Blue Line to Art District station, 5-minute walk
  • Bus: Routes 24, 35, and 67 stop directly in front of the gallery
  • Bike: Bike racks available at the entrance

Parking Options

  • Street Parking: Metered parking available on Art District Boulevard and surrounding streets
  • Creative Quarter Garage: 2 blocks away at 300 Gallery Avenue, $8 flat rate on evenings and weekends
  • Museum District Lot: 3 blocks away at 150 Culture Street, $5 with gallery validation

Accessibility

PersTenas Art Gallery is fully accessible. We offer wheelchair access via our main entrance, accessible restrooms, and elevators to all floors. Assistive listening devices and large-print exhibition guides are available at the front desk. Service animals are welcome. Please contact us in advance if you have specific accessibility needs so we can ensure the best possible experience.
