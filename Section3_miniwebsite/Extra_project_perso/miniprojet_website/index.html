Welcome to PersTenas Art Gallery

Discover the vibrant world of contemporary art at PersTenas, where creativity knows no bounds. Since 2015, we have been dedicated to showcasing extraordinary works from emerging and established artists across the globe. Our mission is to bridge the gap between artistic vision and passionate collectors, creating meaningful connections through the universal language of art.

Current Exhibitions

Abstract Expressions: A Journey Through Color

Immerse yourself in a mesmerizing collection of abstract paintings that challenge conventional perspectives. This exhibition features 25 stunning pieces from renowned international artists who explore the depths of emotion through bold colors, dynamic compositions, and innovative techniques. Running from January through March 2026.

Sculpted Realities: Contemporary 3D Art

Experience the tactile beauty of modern sculpture in our latest exhibition. From bronze to recycled materials, these three-dimensional masterpieces transform space and perception. Featured artists include Marina Delacroix, James Thornhill, and Sofia Mendez. Each piece tells a unique story about our relationship with form, space, and materiality.

Digital Dreams: The Future of Art

Step into the future with our groundbreaking digital art exhibition. Featuring interactive installations, NFT artwork, and augmented reality experiences, this showcase demonstrates how technology is revolutionizing artistic expression. Perfect for tech enthusiasts and traditional art lovers alike, this exhibition pushes the boundaries of what art can be.

Why Choose PersTenas?

Curated Excellence

Every piece in our gallery is carefully selected by our team of expert curators with decades of combined experience in the art world. We maintain the highest standards of quality and authenticity, ensuring that each work meets our rigorous criteria for artistic merit and cultural significance.

Artist Support

We are passionate about supporting artists at every stage of their career. Our artist residency programs, mentorship opportunities, and fair commission structures ensure that creators receive the recognition and compensation they deserve. When you purchase from PersTenas, you directly support the artistic community.

Collector Services

Whether you're a seasoned collector or purchasing your first piece, our dedicated team provides personalized guidance throughout your journey. We offer authentication certificates, professional framing services, international shipping, and ongoing collection management support to protect your investment.
